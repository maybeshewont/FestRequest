We help awesome music enthusiasts such as yourself; raise funds to throw the epic festivals, concerts, or parties of your dreams

Our Guidelines Man:


1. Funding is for festivals, concerts, or pop-up parties only. We want you to imagine the event of your wildest dreams and have fun with it. We want you to be able to make a statement and have people remember your show.

2. A fest has to have a clear plan, artist, line-ups, venue, and a timeline for completion. No open-ended ideas qualify. We need our organizers to do what their name says, be organized. We need clear cut ideas, no beating around the bush here.

3. Not for charity or cause funding. (Rasing money for Red cross or Kenya / Fund my life). Those are awesome ideas, but not what we do.>
