Event Promotion Duration and Funding Deadline

Your event will have a promotional period when people can GET STOKED! about it. After your goal has been met, the real work begins. Make sure you allocate enough time (at least a month) between the your funding deadline and the actual event. It takes a lot to put on an event, so make sure you’re ready. Only events that meet their goals will be funded so make sure to spread the word. Otherwise nobody gets charged.



Things to consider:
* Venue Costs * Artist(s) Fees * Security * Transportation * Equipment/Amps * Lighting * Insurance

Please don't forget that Fest Request takes a 5% fee when a project gets “Stoked!” The amount is calculated to plan out their costs for correct pricing.
